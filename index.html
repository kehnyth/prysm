About Me

PRYSM

Prysm AI closely follows and adapts to the evolving understanding of mental disorders, providing valuable insights to raise awareness and advance mental health research.
‍
Technology should prioritize connection, empathy, and accessibility for those seeking mental health support.

AI-driven content should provide meaningful insights while reflecting the real challenges of mental health struggles.
‍
The community is essential to shaping Prysm’s mission, fostering conversation, and driving positive change in mental health awareness.

PRYSM AI FOCUS

background

Prysm AI has dedicated expertise in mental health research and AI development, focusing on understanding and addressing a wide range of mental disorders.

Prysm AI provides insights and support for:

Anxiety Disorders (e.g., Generalized Anxiety, Social Anxiety)

Mood Disorders (e.g., Depression, Bipolar Disorder)

Obsessive-Compulsive Disorders

Trauma-Related Disorders (e.g., PTSD)

Neurodevelopmental Conditions (e.g., ADHD, Autism Spectrum Disorders)
